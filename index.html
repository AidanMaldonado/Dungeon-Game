Dungeon Master

Sicordi

A group of nomads roam freely in large groups. They are confined to their individual clans. Many set up camp temporarily and may enter the dungeon for adventure and resources.

-For players who are less active

-Easy

Ergonia

A decent sized town that is home to a number of locals and the occasional traveling merchant. A solid variety of interesting folk will attempt to conquer the dungeon.

-For the average player. The difficulty is based on how you are performing.

-Dynamic(Recommended)

Old Bavaria

Bavaria is a mighty kingdom that sits in the valley just outside of your dungeon. Parties of warriors from the empire routinely enter the dungeon and attempt to conquer it.

-For players who are more active and want extreme difficulty

-Insane
